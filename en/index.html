Two engines, one shortcut

Cloud or fully offline — your call

A switch right in Settings. Both engines are fast and add punctuation — choose what matters more: no disk space used, or no audio sent to the cloud.

Cloud

Groq Whisper

Fast, accurate recognition on Groq's servers (whisper-large-v3-turbo). Nothing to download, zero disk footprint. Needs your own (free) Groq key and a connection.

On-device · offline

GigaAM — on your device

Sber's GigaAM model runs entirely on your device — fast, no internet, no key. Audio never leaves your computer. Native Russian recognition with punctuation out of the box. One-time model download: ~400 MB on Mac, ~200 MB on Windows.

offlineno keyprivateRussian

Why it's rare: native-Russian on-device dictation is an almost empty niche. Western apps target English; GigaAM is Sber's open model, strong on Russian.

Private by default

Audio goes only to Groq for recognition — or nowhere at all with the local engine. Your key is kept in a protected file on your device. No backend, no telemetry, no accounts. Open source under MIT — see for yourself.

How it works

Three steps to finished text

Press the key

The default shortcut is Right Option on Mac, Right Alt on Windows. Your choice of mode: hold while speaking, or press once.

Speak

The menu-bar (tray) icon shows it's recording. Speak naturally — Voica handles the punctuation.

Done

A second later the text is in your active field — and always on the clipboard, just in case.

Under the hood

The details that make it just work

Punctuation out of the box

Periods, commas, question marks, quotes, the letter ё — no manual cleanup.

Term dictionary

Your names and jargon, plus AI fixing: "kubernetis" → Kubernetes.

Auto-insert

Text lands right in the field — or an editable window, your choice.

Dictation history

Every transcript at hand: copy, play back the audio, delete.

Russian & English

Mixed speech is recognized; the UI follows your system language.

Open source

MIT, no cloud dependencies beyond your own Groq.

Install Voica in a minute

Download and open it — drag to Applications (Mac) or run the .exe (Windows) — paste your Groq key, or switch on the local engine and work offline.
